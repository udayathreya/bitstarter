Wild Wirecraft is a start-up retail establishment which makes and sells fashionable/Tribal/Wire wrapped jewellery to women of 21st generation.


We offer individuality and design timeless pieces of jewellery suitable for many occasions. Wire wrapping can create some of the most beautiful and delicate looking pendants and jewellery. A movement to break free from uninspired mass produced junk, Wild Wirecraft is a place where we emphasize on creativity and showcase our original work. We can give you variety with chunky, unusual, beautiful, gently and quirky pieces included in our range. Lovers of art - buy from us to show off your exquisite taste.

We are in the process of setting up an e-commerce model for our business inorder to increase our sales, reach out to more customers and let them enjoy a new and fresh shopping experience. We would be offering additional discounts for customers who order from here - Stay Tuned!
